### [`minlos_theorem`](../../../Minlos/Main.lean#L66) — Theorem

**Statement**: On a space $E$ with `[IsHilbertNuclear E]`, `[SeparableSpace E]`, if $\Phi : E \to \mathbb{C}$ is continuous, positive-definite, and normalized ($\Phi(0) = 1$), then there exists a unique probability measure $\mu$ on $\mathrm{WeakDual}_{\mathbb{R}}\, E$ such that
