## Overview

This file states and proves **Minlos' theorem**: on a nuclear, separable, locally convex space $E$, every continuous positive-definite normalized functional $\Phi : E \to \mathbb{C}$ is the characteristic functional of a unique probability measure $\mu$ on the topological dual $E' = \mathrm{WeakDual}_{\mathbb{R}}\, E$. The proof assembles finite-dimensional Bochner marginals, the Kolmogorov projective extension, a measurable projection from $E \to \mathbb{R}$ to $E'$, and a uniqueness argument. A corollary (`minlos_uniqueness`) extracts the uniqueness statement separately.
