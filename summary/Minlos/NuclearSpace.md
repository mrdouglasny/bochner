## Nuclear Spaces

### [`IsHilbertNuclear`](../../Minlos/NuclearSpace.lean#L74) — Class

**Lean signature**
```lean
class IsHilbertNuclear (E : Type*) [AddCommGroup E] [Module ℝ E]
    [TopologicalSpace E] : Prop
```

**Informal**: A **nuclear space** (in the Hilbert-Schmidt sense) is a locally convex space whose topology is generated by a countable family of Hilbertian seminorms with Hilbert-Schmidt inclusion maps. That is: there exists $(p_n)_{n \in \mathbb{N}}$ such that each $p_n$ is Hilbertian, the topology is given by the seminorms $(p_n)$, and the inclusion $\hat{E}_{p_{n+1}} \to \hat{E}_{p_n}$ is Hilbert-Schmidt for each $n$.

**Reference:** Treves, *Topological Vector Spaces*, Ch. 50, Thm 50.1; Gel'fand-Vilenkin, *Generalized Functions* Vol. 4, Ch. 3-4.
