### [`orthonormal_diag_le_hilbert_trace`](../../../Minlos/SazonovTightness.lean#L871) — Theorem

**Statement**: For an orthonormal family $v : \mathrm{Fin}\, n \to H$ and Hilbert basis $\{b_k\}$, the finite sum of diagonal entries is bounded by the trace:
$$\sum_{j=1}^n \langle v_j, S(v_j) \rangle \leq \sum'_k \langle b_k, S(b_k) \rangle.$$
The proof decomposes $b_k = P(b_k) + Q(b_k)$ via the projection $P$ onto $\mathrm{span}(v)$; the cross term vanishes by Parseval, so the difference $= \sum'_k \langle Q(b_k), S(Q(b_k)) \rangle \geq 0$.

**Proof uses**: `HilbertBasis.hasSum_inner_mul_inner`
